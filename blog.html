About Project Pencils

COMPLETE as of 6/28/16

India has tried and failed to combat its student dropout rate. Under the Right of Children to Free and Compulsory Education Act 2009 (RCFCEA), education has been made free for children from 6 to 14 years of age. However, these incentives have not proved to be successful. The Indian dropout rate for students aged 8-10 is 50% for boys and 58% for girls. This is particularly upsetting when noting that this demographic is the target demographic of the RCFCEA. In fact, 50% of children do not go to school.

Project P.E.N.C.I.L.S. (Providing Educational Necessities to Classrooms in Lacking Schools) is a project that aids such lack educational infrastructure. All of the excess costs such as notebooks and uniforms are annulled because we provide them with these fundamental supplies.
