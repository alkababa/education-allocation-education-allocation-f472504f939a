Our Mission

Education Allocation works to reduce student dropout rates by improving school conditions in developing countries.


About Us

Education Allocation was founded in late 2015. The idea sprung when our founder and current CEO, Sid Rao, was on a family vacation in India. Instead of visiting the tourist spots, he spent more time in the poverty stricken areas. Shocked, he, along with a group of friends, formed Education Allocation.

Education Allocation is completely youth-run. We were formed for a charitable purpose, and we serve a charitable purpose. 100% of the donations goes towards the mission. We take $0 for administrative costs. As a youth-run nonprofit, we have the unique ability to understand the standards of education and how to improve it. We have been given great opportunity, and we wish to give back to the community and to the world.
